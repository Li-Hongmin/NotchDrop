Last updated: November 26, 2024

üîí The Short Version

NotchDrop does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Period.

Your privacy is 100% guaranteed. We don't track you, we don't know who you are, and we don't want to know.

üìã Privacy Commitment

NotchDrop is built with privacy as a fundamental principle, not an afterthought. We believe your data belongs to you, and only you.

Zero Data Collection: NotchDrop operates entirely on your local Mac. Nothing leaves your device.

üîç The Details

1. Data Collection

NotchDrop collects absolutely no data. We do not:

  • Track your mouse movements or cursor position beyond local detection
  • Record which menu bar apps you use
  • Monitor how often you use NotchDrop
  • Collect any personally identifiable information
  • Store usage statistics or analytics
  • Access your files or documents

2. Internet Access

NotchDrop does not require or use any internet connection. The app:

  • Runs entirely offline
  • Never connects to external servers
  • Never sends data over the network
  • Works perfectly without Wi-Fi or internet

3. Analytics & Telemetry

We use:

  • No analytics services (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No telemetry (no crash reports, usage data, etc.)
  • No tracking pixels
  • No third-party SDKs

4. Third-Party Services

NotchDrop does not integrate with or use any third-party services. Everything runs locally on your Mac.

5. Local Data Storage

NotchDrop stores only your preferences locally on your Mac using standard macOS UserDefaults:

  • Auto-restore delay setting (5-60 seconds)
  • Launch at login preference (on/off)
  • Automatic detection mode (on/off)

This data never leaves your computer. It's stored in your Mac's local preference files and is never transmitted anywhere.

6. Permissions

NotchDrop requests minimal macOS permissions:

  • Accessibility: To detect mouse position (stays local)
  • Display Management: To switch display modes

These permissions are used only for the app's core functionality and do not involve any data transmission.

7. Open Source Transparency

NotchDrop is open source. You can:

  • Review the complete source code on GitHub
  • Verify there's no tracking or data collection
  • Build the app yourself if you prefer
  • Audit our privacy claims

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/Li-Hongmin/NotchDrop

8. Updates & Changes

If this privacy policy ever changes, we will:

  • Update this page with the new policy
  • Include the date of changes
  • Announce changes in release notes

However, since we don't collect any data, this policy is unlikely to change.

9. Children's Privacy

NotchDrop is safe for users of all ages because we don't collect any data from anyone.

10. Your Rights

Under various privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have rights regarding your data. With NotchDrop:

  • No data to access - we don't store any
  • No data to delete - we don't collect any
  • No data to export - we don't have any
  • No data to correct - we don't maintain any
